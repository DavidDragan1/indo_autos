    Project Title: CarQuest

    This project is a comprehensive online platform designed for buying and selling new and used vehicles (cars and motorbikes), as well as spare parts. It aims to connect buyers and sellers through a user-friendly interface with a wide range of features.

    Key Features of the Platform:

    • Marketplace Functionality: Dedicated sections for buying and selling cars, motorbikes, and spare parts.
    • Search and Filtering: Robust search capabilities with specific filters for each category (make, model, price, condition, location, etc.).
    • Featured Deals and Recent Listings: Highlights attractive offers and the latest inventory across all categories.
    • Seller Profiles: Allows users to register as sellers and manage their listings.
    • Product Listings: Detailed pages for each listing with images, specifications, seller information, and related items.
    • User Accounts: Registration and login for both buyers and sellers.
    • Seller Dashboard: Provides sellers with an overview of their activity, listing statistics, and reporting.
    • Communication Features: Includes a mail system and a chatbox for direct interaction between users.
    • Favourite Listings: Enables users to save and track items they are interested in.
    • Car Valuation Tool: Offers a free service to estimate vehicle values.
    • Vehicle Comparison: Allows users to compare specifications of multiple vehicles side-by-side.
    • Blog: Provides informative articles related to cars, maintenance, and buying/selling.
    • FAQ: Answers frequently asked questions to assist users.
    • Contact and About Us Pages: Provides company information and contact details.

    Support and Updates:

    • Comprehensive Documentation – Step-by-step guides to help you set up and manage ads effortlessly.
    • Regular Updates – New features, performance improvements, and enhancements to keep the platform up to date.
    • Dedicated Support Team – Our team is ready to assist with any issues or queries to ensure a smooth experience.
